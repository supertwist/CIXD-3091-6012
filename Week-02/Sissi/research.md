## Where sources conflict (and why)

### 1) MUSH Vanilla Bean — big calorie/protein swings

*   **210 kcal / 6g protein** appears on Amazon‑style listings for a **141g container**. [\[amazon.com\]](https://www.amazon.com/MUSH-Vanilla-Bean-Overnight-Oats/dp/B08J97BY5J), [\[eatthismuch.com\]](https://www.eatthismuch.com/calories/overnight-oats-vanilla-bean-2442401)
*   **290 kcal / 15g protein** shows up as a **156g container** on FatSecret. [\[foods.fatsecret.com\]](https://foods.fatsecret.com/calories-nutrition/mush/overnight-oats-vanilla-bean)
*   **309 kcal / 9g protein** appears as a **170g container** on MyFoodData. [\[tools.myfooddata.com\]](https://tools.myfooddata.com/nutrition-facts/737062/wt1)

**Most likely explanations:**

1.  **Different product versions or line extensions** (e.g., “Protein” line vs standard), and/or
2.  **Different cup sizes** (141g vs 156g vs 170g) and **data-entry drift** in databases. [\[foods.fatsecret.com\]](https://foods.fatsecret.com/calories-nutrition/mush/overnight-oats-vanilla-bean), [\[amazon.com\]](https://www.amazon.com/MUSH-Vanilla-Bean-Overnight-Oats/dp/B08J97BY5J), [\[tools.myfooddata.com\]](https://tools.myfooddata.com/nutrition-facts/737062/wt1)

**How I handled it:** I reported the conflict explicitly and did **not** force a single “true” number.

[\[brekki.com\]](https://brekki.com/products/vanilla-cinnamon/), [\[fooducate.com\]](https://www.fooducate.com/product/Brekki-Overnight-Oats-Vanilla-Cinnamon/5D917A7E-5FA7-360D-47C1-ADDCA0003FD2)
